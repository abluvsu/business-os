# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** July 9, 2026

## Introduction

Business OS ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy.

Business OS is designed as a **local-first application**. Whenever possible, your data remains on your own device and under your control.

This Privacy Policy explains what information may be processed, how it is used, and your rights.

---

# Information We Collect

Business OS itself does not require an account to use the local application.

Depending on which optional connectors you enable, Business OS may access information from:

- Gmail
- Instagram
- Google Ads
- Websites you choose to analyze
- Other services you explicitly connect

Business OS only accesses data after your explicit authorization.

---

# How We Use Your Data

We use connected data only to:

- Answer your questions
- Generate reports
- Build visualizations
- Understand your business context
- Improve your decision making

We do not sell your personal information.

---

# Local First

Business OS is built around a Local First philosophy.

Your workspace, conversations, reports and business data are stored locally on your device unless you explicitly enable cloud functionality.

You remain the owner of your data.

---

# Third-Party Services

If you connect external services, those services remain governed by their own privacy policies.

Examples include:

- Google
- Meta
- Other connector providers

Business OS only accesses the permissions you grant.

---

# Data Sharing

Business OS does not sell your data.

We do not share your information with third parties except when necessary to provide a connector you explicitly enabled.

---

# Data Security

We strive to protect your information through appropriate technical and organizational safeguards.

However, no software or transmission method is completely secure.

---

# User Data Deletion

Business OS gives you full control over your data.

You may permanently delete your information by:

1. Opening Business OS.
2. Opening Workspace Settings.
3. Choosing **Delete Workspace**.

Alternatively, deleting the Business OS workspace folder from your computer permanently removes locally stored information.

If future cloud services are introduced, corresponding account deletion options will also be provided.

---

# Children's Privacy

Business OS is not intended for children under 13 years of age.

---

# Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.

Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last Updated" date.
