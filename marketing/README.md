# Business OS — Marketing Operating System

> This is not a collection of marketing deliverables. This is the permanent knowledge base that guides every future marketing, branding, SEO, sales, product marketing, content marketing, social media, launch, and growth decision for Business OS.
>
> Future humans and AI agents should be able to read these documents and produce on-brand work without additional prompting.

---

## How to Use This Marketing OS

1. **Before writing anything** (landing page, email, blog post, tweet, ad), read the relevant documents in this repository.
2. **When in doubt about voice**, read `00_brand/07_VOICE_AND_TONE.md` first.
3. **When in doubt about positioning**, read `00_brand/02_POSITIONING.md` first.
4. **When in doubt about who we serve**, read `01_customer/11_ICP.md` first.
5. **When in doubt about what to say**, read `00_brand/08_MESSAGING_PILLARS.md` first.
6. **When in doubt about what words to use**, read `10_assets/approved_phrases.md` and `10_assets/banned_phrases.md`.

## Quality Standards

Every document in this Marketing OS follows a consistent structure:

- **Purpose** — What this document is and why it exists
- **Principles** — The core beliefs that govern this area
- **Best Practices** — How to execute well
- **Examples** — Concrete, production-ready samples
- **Anti-patterns** — What to avoid and why
- **Checklist** — A pre-publish or pre-ship verification list
- **Templates** — Reusable structures for future work
- **Future Updates** — When and how to revise this document
